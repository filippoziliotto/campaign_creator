# Privacy Policy — Campaign Forge

**App name:** Campaign Forge
**Developer:** FZ Labs
**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Campaign Forge is a D&D campaign prompt creator. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Campaign Forge does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking libraries.

## Network Requests

Campaign Forge makes no network requests. All campaign configuration options and prompt generation run entirely on your device using bundled data. No data is ever sent to any server.

## Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using system-provided storage (SharedPreferences):
- Your language preference (Italian or English)
- A draft copy of the last generated prompt, so you can return to it later

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Clipboard

When you use the "Copy prompt" action, the generated prompt text is written to your device clipboard. This is the intended function of the feature and the data is not accessed by the app beyond that action.

## Third-Party Services

Campaign Forge does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

The "Open in ChatGPT" feature opens the ChatGPT website in a browser. The prompt text is placed in your clipboard beforehand. Any interaction with ChatGPT is governed by OpenAI's own privacy policy.

## Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete.

## Children

Campaign Forge does not knowingly collect data from children. No data is collected from any user regardless of age.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to FZ Labs via the app's store listing contact information.
