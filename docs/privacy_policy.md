# Privacy Policy for Campaign Forge

**Last updated:** March 24, 2026

Campaign Forge is a mobile app by FZ Labs for creating tabletop RPG and D&D campaign prompts. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Summary

Campaign Forge is designed to work primarily on-device.

- We do not collect personal information from you.
- The app displays occasional interstitial ads via the Google AdMob SDK. You can remove ads with a one-time in-app purchase.
- We do not sell your data.
- Your generated prompts and preferences are stored locally on your device unless you choose to copy, share, or open external services yourself.

## Information We Collect

Campaign Forge does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, payment information, or account credentials.

The app does not require account creation.

## Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide core functionality, Campaign Forge may store limited app data locally on your device using system-provided storage.

This may include:

- your selected app language
- your selected theme preference
- your saved prompt draft
- the saved timestamp of that draft
- lightweight app state used to determine whether to show the in-app review prompt after repeated successful prompt generations
- a counter of successful prompt generations used to determine when to show interstitial ads
- whether you have purchased the ad-free upgrade

This information remains on your device and is not sent to FZ Labs by the app.

## Prompt Generation

Campaign Forge generates prompts locally on the device using bundled templates and app assets. Your campaign inputs and generated text are not transmitted to FZ Labs servers by the app.

## Clipboard, Sharing, and External Actions

Campaign Forge includes features that act only when you choose to use them:

- **Copy prompt:** writes the generated prompt to your device clipboard
- **Share prompt:** passes the generated prompt to the system share sheet so you can send it using other apps installed on your device
- **Open in ChatGPT:** opens `https://chatgpt.com/` in your browser and copies the generated prompt to your clipboard first for your convenience
- **Store/review links:** may open the Google Play Store or Apple App Store externally when you choose review or sharing actions related to the app listing

Once you share data with another app, browser, or service, that third party handles the data according to its own terms and privacy policy.

## Internet Access

The Android app includes internet permission so external links and platform-integrated features can work when you use them, such as opening ChatGPT in a browser, opening store pages, or showing a platform review flow.

Campaign Forge itself is not designed to upload your prompt content to FZ Labs servers.

## Advertising

Campaign Forge displays interstitial ads after every fifth successful prompt generation using the Google AdMob SDK. Ad requests may transmit your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and general device information to Google for the purpose of serving ads. Google may use this information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

You can permanently remove all ads with a one-time in-app purchase ("Go Ad-Free"). Once the purchase is active on your device, no ad requests are made. If you reinstall the app or clear its data, you can restore your purchase from the app settings to re-enable the ad-free experience.

Campaign Forge does not include third-party analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs beyond what the advertising SDK requires to serve ads.

## In-App Purchases

Campaign Forge offers a one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase to remove ads. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. FZ Labs does not receive or store your payment information. Purchase status is cached locally on your device and can be restored via the "Restore purchases" option in the app settings.

## Third-Party Services

The app may rely on operating-system or store-managed services for actions such as:

- opening web pages
- opening app store pages
- showing an in-app review prompt
- sharing content through the system share sheet
- serving interstitial ads (Google AdMob)
- processing in-app purchases (Apple App Store / Google Play Store)

If you use the optional ChatGPT shortcut, your interaction with ChatGPT is governed by OpenAI's privacy practices, not by FZ Labs.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because Campaign Forge does not collect personal data on behalf of FZ Labs, there is no server-side user data retained by us.

Locally stored app data can typically be removed by:

- clearing the app's local data from your device settings
- uninstalling the app
- deleting saved drafts from within the app, where that option is available

## Children's Privacy

Campaign Forge is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact information provided on the app's store listing.
